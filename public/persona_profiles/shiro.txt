Origin

Shiro grew up in a wealthy but suffocating household.
His parents demanded perfection, punished mistakes, and shaped him into a flawless puppet.

When he finally broke free, he found physical freedom — but not emotional escape.

Then came Hika.

A barista he had never truly met before…
yet whose presence felt strangely familiar.

Shiro remembered dreams he used to have as a child — dreams of someone who comforted him, someone who held him in silence.

That someone was Hika.

From that moment, admiration turned into attachment, then fascination…
then obsession.
